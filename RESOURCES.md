# Track 1: Cybersecurity

## Knowledge

### Foundational Books
- **"Security Engineering" by Ross Anderson (3rd ed., free online)** — https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~rja14/book.html — The definitive academic textbook on security engineering. Covers history, threat modeling, cryptography, network security, psychology of security, and economics of security. High trust; written by a Cambridge professor.
- **"Hacking: The Art of Exploitation" by Jon Erickson** — Explains how attacks actually work at a low level. Useful for understanding why defenses matter.
- **"The Art of Invisibility" by Kevin Mitnick** — Accessible introduction to modern privacy and security threats from a celebrated former hacker.

### Frameworks & Standards
- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF 2.0)** — https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework — The US government's authoritative framework. Five functions: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover. Essential vocabulary for any presales conversation.
- **MITRE ATT&CK** — https://attack.mitre.org — The industry-standard knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques. Every SOC/SIEM vendor references it.
- **OWASP Top 10** — https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/ — The canonical list of web application security risks. Essential for application security presales.

### Academic / Research
- **IEEE Security & Privacy** — https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/xpl/RecentIssue.jsp?punumber=8013 — Peer-reviewed journal; cutting-edge research.
- **ACM CCS (Conference on Computer and Communications Security)** — https://dl.acm.org/conference/ccs — Top academic security conference proceedings.
- **USENIX Security Symposium** — https://www.usenix.org/conferences/byname/108 — Top practitioner-academic conference; many papers are freely available.

### News & Ongoing Learning
- **Krebs on Security** — https://krebsonsecurity.com — Highest-trust cybersecurity journalism. Brian Krebs investigates real breaches and threat actors.
- **Schneier on Security** — https://www.schneier.com — Bruce Schneier's blog; covers policy, cryptography, and security thinking. Weekly newsletter.
- **The Hacker News** — https://thehackernews.com — Fast news on vulnerabilities, breaches, and product announcements.

### Courses
- **MIT 6.858 Computer Systems Security** — https://css.csail.mit.edu/6.858/ — Free lecture videos and notes from MIT's security course. High academic rigor.
- **Stanford Cryptography I (Coursera)** — https://www.coursera.org/learn/crypto — The best free cryptography course available; taught by Dan Boneh.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- **r/netsec** (Reddit) — https://www.reddit.com/r/netsec/ — High-quality technical security community. Good for staying current.
- **SANS Internet Storm Center** — https://isc.sans.edu — Daily threat intelligence digests from practitioners.
- **DEF CON / Black Hat talks (YouTube)** — Annual conference talks, freely available. Mix of cutting-edge research and practitioner wisdom.

## Gaps

- No strong free resource yet identified for cybersecurity presales-specific content (how to translate technical concepts into buyer value). Will add as found.

# Track 2: 资产配置（个人财富管理）

## Knowledge

### Foundational Books
- **William Bernstein, 《聪明的资产配置者》** (The Intelligent Asset Allocator) — 将学术理论转化为个人投资者可执行的方案。最适合非专业人士入门。强烈推荐。
- **David Swensen, 《机构投资的创新之路》** (Pioneering Portfolio Management) — 耶鲁捐赠基金CIO的经典著作，介绍机构级资产配置框架。
- **Roger Gibson, 《资产配置》** (Asset Allocation: Balancing Financial Risk) — 系统全面的教科书，适合深入学习。
- **Burton Malkiel, 《漫步华尔街》** (A Random Walk Down Wall Street) — 从有效市场假说角度论证被动指数投资的合理性。

### Academic / Research
- **Brinson, Hood & Beebower (1991)** "Determinants of Portfolio Performance II" — Financial Analysts Journal — https://doi.org/10.2469/faj.v47.n3.38 — 奠基性研究，"资产配置决定90%回报"的一手来源。
- **Damodaran Online（NYU）** — https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/ — 各资产类别历史回报、风险溢价等数据的权威来源。免费。
- **Vanguard Research** — https://institutional.vanguard.com/content/dam/inst/vanguard-has/insights-pdfs/research-portal — 持续发布高质量投资研究报告，中立可信。

### Free Guides & Reports
- **Vanguard《投资成功原则》** — https://www.vanguard.com/pdf/ISGGPEA.pdf — 免费PDF，涵盖资产配置基础原则。高度推荐作为入门读物。
- **Morningstar Investment Research Center** — https://www.morningstar.com — 基金数据和分析；部分内容免费。

### Classic Portfolio Models (for future lessons)
- **Ray Dalio 全天候组合** (All Weather Portfolio): 30% 股票 / 40% 长期债券 / 15% 中期债券 / 7.5% 黄金 / 7.5% 大宗商品
- **Harry Browne 永久投资组合** (Permanent Portfolio): 各25%等分于股票、长期国债、黄金、现金
- **传统60/40组合**: 60% 全球股票 + 40% 投资级债券

## Wisdom (Communities)

- **Bogleheads** — https://www.bogleheads.org/forum/ — 全球最高质量的个人投资者社区，奉行低成本指数投资哲学。活跃且严谨。
- **r/personalfinance** (Reddit) — https://www.reddit.com/r/personalfinance/ — 大众个人理财讨论，质量参差但量大。
- **晨星中国** — https://cn.morningstar.com — 中文基金数据与分析，适合关注A股和国内基金。
