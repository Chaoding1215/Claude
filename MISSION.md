# Mission: Cybersecurity

## Why
To build broad, credible cybersecurity fluency for a presales role — enabling confident technical conversations with buyers across any solution area: endpoint, network, cloud, identity, application security, and SOC/SIEM.

## Success looks like
- Can explain the history and evolution of cybersecurity to a customer without notes
- Can articulate core concepts (CIA triad, threat modeling, attack surface, zero trust, etc.) in customer-friendly language
- Can speak credibly about modern threat categories and how vendors address them
- Can discuss cutting-edge research topics (AI in security, post-quantum cryptography, etc.) at a level that impresses technical buyers
- Can name the major open challenges the industry hasn't solved yet

## Constraints
- Learning is session-based; must build cumulatively across sessions
- Goal is fluency for conversation, not hands-on technical skills (no CTF, no lab work required)

## Out of scope
- Deep exploit development or offensive techniques
- Certification exam prep (CISSP, CEH, etc.) as a primary goal
- Vendor-specific product training
